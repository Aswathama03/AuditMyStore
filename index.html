FAQ

Common Questions

Does it work on any Shopify store?+
Yes! Quick Analyze works on any public Shopify store — even competitors'. For real revenue and order data, connect your own store via OAuth.
Who is Debbie?+
Debbie is your AI Shopify consultant powered by advanced AI. She analyses your store data and gives specific, revenue-focused recommendations. Think of her as a senior e-commerce consultant available 24/7.
How accurate is the free analysis?+
The free analysis uses publicly available data and AI estimates based on industry benchmarks. Revenue figures are estimates — connect your store for real numbers. The issues, quick wins, and blueprint are based on actual store data.
Is my store data safe?+
We only request read-only access. We never store your products, customer data, or orders — only the access token needed to fetch analytics. Revoke access anytime from your Shopify admin.
Can I cancel my monthly subscription?+
Yes, cancel anytime from your account dashboard. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
